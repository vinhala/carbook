For the 2018 Tacoma 4WD front differential, here are the specifications from the manual:

  • Drain Plug Torque 48 ft-lbf (65 N·m)
  • Fill Plug Torque 29 ft-lbf (39 N·m)
  • Fluid Type Toyota Genuine Differential Gear Oil LT 75W-85 GL-5

Remember to always remove the fill plug before the drain plug to ensure you can refill it.
