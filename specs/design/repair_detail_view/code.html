Brake Pad Replacement

warning High Urgency
schedule Updated 2 days ago

Description

Replacing the factory front brake pads with high-performance ceramic pads to improve stopping power and reduce brake dust. The current pads are showing significant wear and emit a slight squeal during heavy braking. This modification will also enhance heat dissipation during prolonged downhill descents.

Notes

Ensure calipers are thoroughly cleaned and sliding pins are greased before installing the new pads. Check rotor thickness to confirm they are within spec.
